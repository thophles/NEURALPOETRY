Neural Poetry is an ongoing experiment that acts as a collaboration between poet and machine. Using the Char-RNN algorithm, the works of multiple hip-hop emcees, poets, and philosphers are sourced for training and once a body of work has been generated, it is minimally edited down for literacy, and trained again on the new material. This site includes those results, as well as my own work that went into training it.

Technologies: Python, Javascript, HTML, CSS
